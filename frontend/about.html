About Ally - AI Meeting Wizard

Your intelligent partner for productive meetings.

Our Mission

At Ally, we're dedicated to transforming how teams collaborate. We believe that meetings should be a catalyst for progress, not a drain on time and energy. Our mission is to provide powerful, intuitive tools that handle the administrative burdens of meetings—like transcription, summarization, and action item tracking—so you and your team can focus on what truly matters: innovation and decision-making. By leveraging the power of artificial intelligence, we make every meeting more efficient, actionable, and valuable.

How to Use This Thing

Hey there! Getting started with the wizard is pretty straightforward. I have provided a brief explanation below for you.

Step 1: Give It Something to Work With

First, you need to give the AI some text from your meeting. Head over to the Home page. You've got a few options:

  • Upload a file: Got a pdf, txt, docx, an audio or video recording? Just drag it into the box or click to upload.
  • Record audio: You can either dictate live into the text box with your microphone or record your screen and system audio directly (useful for online meetings).
  • Paste text: If you already have notes, just copy and paste them into the big text editor on the right. Remember, the more context you provide, the better the AI can summarize!
    You can ask Ally questions about the text you pasted in, or the file you uploaded, by clicking on the "Ask Ally about the Transcript" button below the text editor.

Once your text is in there, hit the big "Process Content" button.

Step 2: Get Your Summary

The app will take you to a new page where you can choose a role or a language if you want a specific kind of summary. Most of the time, you can just leave this as is and click "Get Result." The AI will get you a summary and a list of action items for you. You can click back to regenerate another summary if you want to try a different approach, its up to you. You can also ask Ally questions about the summary by typing in the input box at the bottom of the page.

Step 3: Check Out the Results

After processing, you can find everything neatly organized:

  • Action Items: Click on Manage Action logs button or "Action Items" in the sidebar. This is where you'll see a to-do list pulled from your meeting. You can edit tasks, assign them to people, and track their status.
  • History: The "History" page saves all your past meetings—transcripts, summaries, and all. So you can always go back and find what you need.

And that's pretty much it! Just feed it your meeting info, and let the wizard do the heavy lifting.

Just a quick note, you can also get summary of other documents not just meeting transcripts.
